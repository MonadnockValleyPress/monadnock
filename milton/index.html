Monadnock Valley Press: John Milton

The Monadnock Valley Press has republished the epic poem Paradise Lost by John Milton (1608-1674), as well as his pamphlet Of Education and his defense of free speech, Areopagitica. In addition, we have republished the following sonnets:

  • On His Deceased Wife
  • On His Blindness
  • On His Having Arrived at the Age of Twenty-Three
  • On Shakespear
  • To Cyriack Skinner
  • To Mr. H. Lawes on His Airs
  • To Mr. Lawrence
  • To the Nightingale
