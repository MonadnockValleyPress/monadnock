Monadnock Valley Press: Alfred Tennyson

The Monadnock Valley Press has republished the poetry collection In Memoriam A. H. H. by Alfred Tennyson (1809-1892), along with the following single poems:

  • Break, Break, Break
  • The Charge of the Light Brigade
  • Crossing the Bar
  • Lucretius
  • Ring Out, Wild Bells
  • Tears, Idle Tears
  • Tithonus
  • Two Voices
  • Ulysses
  • To Victor Hugo
