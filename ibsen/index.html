Monadnock Valley Press: Henrik Ibsen

The Monadnock Valley Press is in the process of republishing all of the modern prose dramas by Henrik Ibsen; so far we have published the first four:

  • Pillars of Society (1877)
  • A Doll's House (1879)
  • Ghosts (1881)
  • An Enemy of the People (1882)
  • The Wild Duck (1884)
  • Rosmersholm (1886)
  • The Lady from the Sea (1898)
  • Hedda Gabler (1890)
  • The Master Builder (1892)
  • Little Eyolf (1894)
  • John Gabriel Borkman (1896)
  • When We Dead Awaken (1899)
