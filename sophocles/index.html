Monadnock Valley Press: Sophocles

The Monadnock Valley Press is in the process of republishing English translations of the extant plays written by the ancient Greek dramatist Sophocles (c. 497-406 BCE):

  • Ajax
  • Antigone
  • Electra
  • Oedipus at Colonus
  • Oedipus the King
  • Philoctetes
  • Women of Trachis
