Monadnock Press: Frederick Douglass

The Monadnock Press has republished two public-domain works by Frederick Douglass:

  • John Brown (delivered at the fourteenth anniversary of Storer College, Harper's Ferry, West Virginia, on May 30, 1881)
  • Narrative of the Life of Frederick Douglass, An American Slave
  • Oration in Memory of Abraham Lincoln (delivered at the unveiling of The Freedmen's Monument in Memory of Abraham Lincoln, Lincoln Park, Washington, D.C., on April 14, 1876)
  • Self-Made Men (delivered to the Indian Industrial School of Carlisle, Pennsylvania, in 1872)
