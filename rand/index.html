Monadnock Valley Press: Ayn Rand

The Monadnock Valley Press has republished one public-domain text by Ayn Rand: her novel Anthem. As explained in the detailed textual notes, this edition restores the definitive text of Anthem for the first time in over 65 years. This book is now also available within the USA in a paperback version, with a Kindle ebook version to follow very soon.
