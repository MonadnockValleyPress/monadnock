Monadnock Valley Press: George Eliot

The Monadnock Valley Press is in the process of republishing the novels of George Eliot (1819-1880):

  • Adam Bede (1859)
  • The Mill on the Floss (1860)
  • Silas Marner (1861)
  • Romola (1862-3)
  • Felix Holt, Radical (1866)
  • Middlemarch (1871-2)
  • Daniel Deronda (1876)

We have also published her Review of Thoreau's Walden and three of her poems: Brother and Sister, The Choir Invisible, and Self and Life.
