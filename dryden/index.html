Monadnock Valley Press: John Dryden

The Monadnock Valley Press has republished the following poems by John Dryden (1631-1700), as well as his translation of Virgil's Aeneid.

  • Alexander's Feast
  • The Fair Stranger
  • Heroic Stanzas on the Death of Oliver Cromwell
  • "I Feed a Flame Within"
  • The Tears of Amynta
