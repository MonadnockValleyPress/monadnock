Monadnock Valley Press

The Monadnock Valley Press is an online publisher of public domain writings that reflect our vision of human potential, with a special focus on the literature of freedom and the classics of Western civilization, the Anglosphere, and America.

We publish works by a wide range of authors in literature, philosophy, history, the sciences, and the arts. To explore further, refer to our lists of autobiographies, dialogues, epics, essays, novels, plays, poems, speeches, and stories.

Past and future publications are catalogued in the chronology and roadmap. The credits might also be of interest.
