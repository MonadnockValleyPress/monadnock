Monadnock Valley Press: Emma Lazarus

The Monadnock Valley Press has republished the following poems by Emma Lazarus (1849-1887), who is best known for having authored the poem that graces the Statue of Liberty in New York Harbor.

  • Afternoon
  • Agamemnon's Tomb
  • Arabesque
  • Autumn Sadness
  • Echoes
  • How Long?
  • Long Island Sound
  • The New Colossus
  • Success
  • Venus of the Louvre
